## 🔧 Reflex & Workflow Optimization

### Overview

This module simulates post-go-live analysis and optimization of laboratory workflows, focusing on identifying root causes of performance issues and implementing workflow-level corrections.

The work reflects how analysts evaluate and improve laboratory systems after initial stabilization, with emphasis on workflow design, reflex testing logic, and operational performance in Epic Beaker–aligned environments.

---

### Objectives

- Identify root causes of persistent workflow delays and inefficiencies  
- Model workflow corrections and optimization strategies  
- Improve turnaround time (TAT) performance through process redesign  
- Simulate refinement of reflex testing logic and workflow dependencies  
- Monitor system performance trends over time  

---

### Key Focus Areas

#### Root Cause Analysis

- Identification of underlying drivers of workflow delays  
- Differentiation between pre-analytic, transport, and analytic issues  
- Analysis of workflow dependencies contributing to inefficiencies  

---

#### Workflow Optimization

- Correction of inefficient or broken process chains  
- Redesign of workflow steps to reduce delays  
- Alignment of workflow behavior with expected system logic  

---

#### Reflex Testing Logic

- Simulation of reflex pathways and conditional testing behavior  
- Identification of gaps or inefficiencies in reflex workflows  
- Impact of order configuration on downstream testing and results  

---

#### Performance Monitoring

- Tracking TAT improvements over time  
- Identification of recurring patterns or regressions  
- Evaluation of optimization effectiveness  

---

### Tools & Technologies

- **SQL** — Root cause analysis and workflow investigation  
- **Python (pandas)** — Simulation of workflow corrections and performance trends  
- **Power BI** — Visualization of optimization impact and performance metrics
